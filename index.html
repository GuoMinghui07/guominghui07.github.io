Minghui Guo

I am an MSc student in AI for Science at the Faculty of Science, National University of Singapore; I am currently a research intern at the NUS Center for Trusted Internet and Community (CTIC), receiving day-to-day mentorship from Dr. Hao Fei and Dr. Shengqiong Wu, and faculty supervision from Prof. Mong-Li Lee and Prof. Wynne Hsu. I received my B.Eng. in Mechatronics Engineering from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2025, and my research interests include unified any-to-any multimodal foundation models, representation learning, and video understanding and generation, as well as AI for Science and data-driven scientific discovery.
